About La Marketa

Three shops in one stop.

The Little Marketa is Plano's neighborhood mercado — part carniceria, part Mexican grocery, part hot-food counter. Walk in for a pound of carne asada, walk out with tortillas, salsa, and a tray of pozole for the family.
